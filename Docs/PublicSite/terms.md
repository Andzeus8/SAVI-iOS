# SAVI Terms Of Use Draft

Last updated: May 9, 2026

This is a publication-ready draft for the first SAVI beta. Review it against
the exact submitted build, public domain, company/legal details, and App Store
requirements before publishing.

## Use Of SAVI

SAVI is for saving and organizing personal content you choose to save. You are
responsible for the content you save and any content you choose to publish if
public sharing is enabled.

## No Medical, Legal, Or Financial Advice

SAVI may help you save articles, research, links, and notes. SAVI does not
provide medical, legal, or financial advice.

## Public Sharing

If public sharing launches, you may only publish content you have the right to
share. SAVI may remove content, restrict accounts, or hide public links that
violate community guidelines, law, rights, or platform rules.

## Private Content

SAVI is designed to keep private content private. You should still avoid storing
content in SAVI that you cannot safely keep on your device or backups.

## Service Changes

SAVI may change features, samples, social tools, sync, or analytics as the
product evolves.
