# SAVI Community Guidelines Draft

These guidelines apply if SAVI launches public profiles, public links, or friend
feeds.

## Allowed In Social V1

- Public web links you choose to share.
- Public titles/captions that are safe, legal, and non-abusive.
- Following friends by username.
- Saving a friend's public link into your private library.

## Not Allowed

- Harassment or threats.
- Hate or abuse.
- Sexual content involving minors.
- Doxxing or private personal information.
- Malware, phishing, scams, or credential theft.
- Copyright-infringing content.
- Public screenshots, PDFs, private files, IDs, Private Vault content, or private
  documents.
- Medical, legal, or financial claims presented as professional advice.

## Safety Controls

SAVI social must include:

- report,
- block,
- delete/unpublish,
- moderation review,
- support contact.
